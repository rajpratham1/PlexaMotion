PlexaMotion

What does PlexaMotion mean?

A blend of **Plexus** (a network) and **Motion**. It signifies a system that seamlessly processes the complex network of human motion to enable fluid, digital creation.

Unlock the power of touchless creativity! **PlexaMotion** is a cutting-edge web tool that lets you draw and interact using simple hand gestures. Say goodbye to mice and keyboards‚Äîjust move your hand and watch your ideas come to life on the digital canvas.

How to Use ‚ñº

How to Use (English)

  • Select Action: In the writer window, move your hand to the top of the screen and point your index finger at a color or button (Erase, Clear).
  • Draw: Keep your middle finger down while raising your index finger to draw.
  • Change Brush Size: Point your index finger at the "Size" slider at the top right to adjust the thickness.
  • Upload Background: Use the controls at the bottom of the writer page to upload an image/PDF or switch to a whiteboard/blackboard.
