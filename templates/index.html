What does PlexaMotion mean?

┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а

A blend of **Plexus** (a network) and **Motion**. It signifies a system that seamlessly processes the complex network of human motion to enable fluid, digital creation.

┬а ┬а
┬а ┬а
┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а

Unlock the power of touchless creativity! **PlexaMotion** is a cutting-edge web tool that lets you draw and interact using simple hand gestures. Say goodbye to mice and keyboardsтАФjust move your hand and watch your ideas come to life on the digital canvas.

How to Use (English)

┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
    ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
  • Select Action: Hold your hand up and point your index finger at a color or button (Erase, Clear) in the top bar.
    • ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
  • Draw: Form a **pinching gesture** (index finger and thumb close together) while moving your hand to draw.
    • ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
  • Change Brush Size: Use the size slider at the top right.
    • ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
  • Erase: Select the **ERASE** button and draw to clear the lines.
    • ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а ┬а
